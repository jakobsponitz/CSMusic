About Us

For over 20 years, despite the constantly changing musical climate, C&S Music Entertainment has always had the ability to stand out from the crowd with the combination of creativity, and ability to hand-pick unique and talented musicians. We play international music including Russian, American, Jewish and Italian. Most important, C&S Music Entertainment stands out when it comes to the personal touch that we give to each of our clients.
